Join Us

Come and join FORCE North for its Club Soccer Tryouts. Identify which age group your boy or girl player(s) fall into to find the one date, time and location of upcoming tryouts that is most convenient for you. Then, follow the instructions to register and fill out the forms referenced in the Tryout Essentials section at the bottom of the page before attending the tryouts. FORCE North looks forward to seeing you there.

In the unlikely event of rain, San Mateo Parks and Recreation may close Bay Meadows Park fields. To find out if the fields are closed, call the Parks and Rec hotline (650) 522-7439. If the fields do close on the date you were going to attend, please use one of the remaining dates for your age group.

All players must register online in advance of attending the tryout.

Tryout Essentials

  • Online Registration: All players must register online in advance of attending the tryout.
  • Participation Forms: All players should download, fill out, and bring a Medical Information and Liability Release form to their tryout session.
  • Attendance: In order to be properly assessed by the coach, players must arrive before the scheduled start time, in proper attire, and with forms filled out in advance.
  • Attire: Players should be dressed to play in shin guards, appropriate cleats (no baseball cleats permitted), long socks, shorts and a t-shirt.
  • Water and Jacket: Please also put water and a jacket in your childâ€™s backpack.

 

NOTE: Please arrive 30 minutes prior to your tryout session to allow time for player check-in and warm up.

Tryout Results

Tryout decisions will be made within a week of the tryout sessions. Each Age Group Coordinator or head age group coach will make individual phone calls to the families of all players invited to join the club. This conversation gives families the opportunity to receive feedback and ask questions.
