tryout

play
noun try·out \ˈtrī-ˌau̇t\

Simple Definition of tryout

  • : a test of someone's ability to do something that is used to see if he or she should join a team, perform in a play, etc.

  • : a test of the quality, value, or usefulness of something

Source: Merriam-Webster's Learner's Dictionary
  •  
  •  
  •  
  •  

Full Definition of tryout

  1. :  an experimental performance or demonstration: asa :  a test of the ability (as of an athlete or actor) to fill a part or meet standardsb :  a performance of a play prior to its official opening to determine response and discover weaknesses
