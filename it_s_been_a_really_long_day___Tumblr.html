            I really love Bo Burnham’s Make Happy show but I also hate it because it really made me think and reevaluate my entire life

            like his message of it not being his job to fix people or make them happy and how artists take on the role and it’s not their right - when I think about what I want to do with my creative career it’s to do everything he said was not my job 

            and I guess I thought that was my job, I thought that was the job of all people who are given a voice in the world - like in some ways, you do have a duty to entertain, educate, and ultimately make people happy - but not permanently 

            people who really need help aren’t going to be fixed by watching someone on TV go through the same thing they are

            they might feel better and they might feel relieved that someone else is going through it and they’re not alone, but that’s not going to fix their problems

            I guess I kind of wanted it to

            but to think it’s my job to create something that can Heal like that it’s stressful and it’s scary because what if you fail? 

            and I think that’s exactly what Bo Burnham was addressing in his show- that he can think he has the power to Help that much but he doesn’t and thinking that only stifles him because he has to take on the huge burden of Saving the masses when he’s just one guy trying to figure out how to be happy himself
