Tryouts Volunteer

As with any club, Seattle United relies on our phenomenal parent group to volunteer at club events. Without this assistance, Seattle United would not be able to organize and execute a large event like tryouts. Please consider volunteering on behalf of all Seattle United players!
Learn More

Medical Release Form

Washington Youth Soccer, the Seattle Youth Soccer Association and Seattle United require that all players have a current medical release form on hand at all events. Both new and returning players will need to bring a completely filled out, signed copy of the form with them to tryouts and submit at check in.
Open Form

Parent Info

Each year, Seattle United hosts a New Parent Information Night just prior to the U8-U10 tryout sessions. This presentation covers many different areas and, while not mandatory, is recommended for all new players interested in trying out at Seattle United.
