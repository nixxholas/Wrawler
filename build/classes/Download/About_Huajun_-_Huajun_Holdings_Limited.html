About Huajun / 1.1 Corporate Profile

About Huajun

Huajun Holdings Limited (stock code: 0377, HK Exchange), formerly known as New Island Printing Holdings Limited as well as New Island Development Holdings Limited, was established by Madam So Chau Yim Ping and a partner in the name of 新洲印刷公司 located in Wan Chai, Hong Kong to carry out small-sized printing business. The company finally occupied the leading position of printing industry in Hong Kong and even Asia after over two decades’ development. New Island Printing Holdings Limited waslisted on the Main Board of Hong Kong Exchange on 19 April 1993. Withstanding all the trials and challenges in the past fifty years, New Island Development Holdings Limited not only developed into a multinational corporation with its products available in Europe and America, but also made its way to be a professional investment holdings company through reorganization and changing ownership of shareholding.

Huajun International Limited held by Mr. Meng Guang Bao purchased 62.62% shareholdings of New Island Holdings in August 2014, which therefore became the substantial shareholder of New Island Development Holdings Limited. In December 2014, New Island Development Holdings Limited formally changed its name to Huajun Holdings Limited.

Huajun Holdings will inherit the core value guide of “Indeed Being and Pragmatic Acting”. We persistently hold market-oriented strategy and aim to build Huajun Holdings as a super international investment holdings company by deploying a multi-level and echeloned layout under the notion of ＂State under Foot, World in Sight＂ by virtue of our great intelligence, powerful strength as well as elite management team with vast experience and spirit of adhering the times. The business of Huajun Holdings can be classified into six categories: industrial investment, medical investment, financial investment, property investment and asset management.

Industrial investment has always been the core business of our company. Based on its fifty-year history in printing branded New Island Printing, Huajun explores the investment opportunities derived from the upstream and downstream of its mainly involved industries including printing, water supplying, energy and environment protection by means of investment holdings as well as merger and reorganization, and set its sight on the excellent enterprises both in China and abroad for investing.

Medical investment business is the field where our company makes great efforts to develop in response to the Ministry of Health’s call of developing the medical and healthcare greatly so as to improve the medical and health service quality for the citizens in respect of hospital reform. The company absorbs those outstanding regional medical institutions that have completed the shareholding system transformation through direct acquisition or entering into investment management agreement, and creates a national leading medical management company with the mode of unified management, adding investment, resources and profit sharing.

Financial investment business mainly engages in providing the customers with various financial services through the subsidiaries of the holding company, which currently includes financial leasing, logistics finance, mortgage loan and so on.

Property investment segment consists of land consolidation and development, real estate development and sales, propertrried out by the managements based on their rich investing experience. It will search steady income with controllable risk, decentralize the company’s operation risk, improve the financial liy management, and various real estate investing business. It will utilize the holding company’s rich domestic resources to seek for development projects with appreciation potential for investing so as to capture the asset increment while keep the steady profit.

Securities management business mainly refers to the short- and med-term investments with the company’s free capital caquidity as well as enhance the solvency of the company.

Trade and logistics, mainly through agents world-class leading manufacturers of high-end electronic parts and the ability to do the Syrian commodities, combined with logistics finance the creation of value for shareholders.

Energy and power, through into the clean energy industry, to seize the strong development of the concept of environmental protection, to create a green future.
