PINK - Try lyrics

Lyrics » A-Z » Pink

Pop rebel singer Pink has selected the track never-give-up anthem "Try" as the official second single from her sixth studio album "The Truth About Love", due for release on September 18th via RCA Records! Produced by Greg Kurstin, "Try" is an brilliantly-written rock mid-tempo that speaks about taking risks. The melody is to die for!
