Book IELTS

Book an IELTS test in your country. Tests are available at locations all over the world.

IELTS is the world’s leading English language test with over two million tests taken in the last year. Take your IELTS test with British Council and gain your passport to international opportunity and global mobility.

Find your nearest IELTS test location with our interactive search facility and take the IELTS test in the country of your choice.

You will find all you need to book your test.

Please note: If you want to register to take an IELTS test for UK Visa and Immigration purposes then please visit Book IELTS for UKVI.
