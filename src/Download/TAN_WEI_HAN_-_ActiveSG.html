TAN WEI HAN

Sport

Badminton

Gender

Female

ABOUT ME

My love for this sport started because my 2 sisters used to play badminton as well & also I got good at it.

After a long competition, there are three things I do: hug my cat, spend time with my family and have a sumptuous meal. I have resided in Tampines since I was born. Shopping is hassle-free in my neighbourhood with its three shopping malls. 

SPORTING ACHIEVEMENTS

  • 2015 SEA Games, Women's Team - Bronze
  • 2015 Vietnam International Challenge - XD SF
  • 2015 Thailand International Challenge  XD QF
  • 2015 Malaysia Masters Grand Prix Gold - XD Last 16
  • 2014 Singapore International Series - XD Champion
  • 2014 Malaysia International Challenge - XD Runner up
