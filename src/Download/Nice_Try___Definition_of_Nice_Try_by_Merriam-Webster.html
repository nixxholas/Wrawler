nice try

Definition of nice try

  1. :  a good effort or attempt to do something <Nice try. I'm sure you'll do better next time.> —usually used in an ironic way to say that an attempt at something was not very good or was not a nice thing to do <Nice try, but you're not going to trick me this time.>
