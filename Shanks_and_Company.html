The Experience
to Move you

North Belfast's leading estate agency offers a high quality house selling and property rental service.
Shanks and Company Estate Agents prides itself in offering clients a high standard of care and professional advice throughout the house selling, house buying and property letting process.
If you want to sell or rent a property, we offer a no obligation market appraisal to determine the best possible selling price or what rental income your property could achieve in the current market.
Shanks and Company is owned by Barbara Shanks who has worked in the North Belfast area for over 25 years; Barbara set up her own estate agency in 2004 to service North Belfast and surrounding areas and now has a significant presence in Templepatrick, Dunadry, Crumlin and Parkgate areas.
Our rental specialists offer an initial rental appraisal; once we have found a suitable tenant we will obtain references, draw up a legally binding tenancy agreement, arrange standing orders and collect the deposit with the first month's rent.
We also offer an in house mortgage advice service to assist anyone who is either purchasing for the first time, moving property, buy to let investors or someone who wants to review their existing mortgage arrangements. There is no additional cost for using this service.
