Making products more flavorful. Making brands more successful.

Shank’s products are used as flavoring ingredients and coloring components for commercial food & beverage production, by culinary professionals and in contract manufacturing & private label packaging for retail & grocery businesses. Shank’s has become the flavor and extract supplier of choice for many brands because we provide superior products … with an emphasis on quality, flavor, customer service and value. We offer standard products and custom formulations.
