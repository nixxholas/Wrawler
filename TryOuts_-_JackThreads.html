    How TRYOUTS work

    • 1 |  Browse

      Choose items to try — and pay nothing upfront.

    • 2 |  Tryout

      Try things on at home for free. You'll have a week to decide what to keep.

      Your TryOut comes with everything you need to send stuff back — for free. No need for a printer, packing tape, or pre-scheduled pick-up. Done.
