Tryouts

Primary Tryouts

Primary Tryouts for the Fall 2016/Spring 2017 season for Select, Travel & Developmental Academy have been posted to each dedicated page

The Soccer Association of Columbia runs 3 tryout-based programs:

  • Select
  • Travel
  • Developmental Academy/PreAcademy

Each of these 3 programs hold separate team or age based tryouts, through which team selection is determined. To view tryout dates & times for any of these programs, please follow the appropriate link above.

All tryout selections will be posted within the corresponding tryout pages found above.

Supplemental Tryouts

All Supplemental Tryouts for the Soccer Association of Columbia will be posted to the “Tryout Dates” section of our homepage. No Supplemental Tryout dates or results will be posted to the Primary Tryout pages listed above.
